Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating and implementing new ideas, methods, or products to solve problems or improve efficiency. It drives progress and fosters growth by challenging existing norms and finding better solutions.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs. It emphasizes balancing environmental, social, and economic priorities for long-term well-being.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental systems and structures, such as transportation, energy, and communication networks, that support the functioning of a society or economy. It plays a critical role in enabling growth, connectivity, and quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of enabling individuals or groups to take control of their lives and make decisions that shape their future. It fosters confidence, self-reliance, and the ability to overcome challenges.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus, Nobel laureate, recently outlined his vision for a “Bangladesh 2.0,” emphasizing justice, human rights, and freedom of speech. Speaking at the 'Bay of Bengal Conversation - 2024,' he highlighted the resilience of the nation following the historic July uprising, which claimed 1,500 lives and led to the fall of the previous regime. Dr. Yunus stressed the importance of rebuilding systems eroded by corruption, addressing economic disparity, and tackling climate challenges. He urged international collaboration and praised the youth's role in shaping the nation's future. The interim government, he stated, is committed to establishing free, fair elections and rebuilding an inclusive, democratic society. Plans include a memorial for uprising victims and reforms to ensure sustainable development.
